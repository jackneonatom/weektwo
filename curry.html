CURRY CHICKEN IS THE BEST

Picture succulent pieces of chicken, marinated in a blend of Jamaican herbs and spices, creating a burst of flavors in every bite. The chicken is then cooked to perfection, simmering in a rich and fragrant curry sauce that is made bright yellow with Jamaican curry powder. As the curry chicken cooks, the enticing aroma fills the air, creating an atmosphere that is both inviting and mouthwatering. The dish is often accompanied by the addition of carrots and white potatoes, which add a delightful texture and complement the flavors of the curry. With each spoonful, you'll experience a symphony of tastes, from the warmth of the spices to the tender juiciness of the chicken. The curry sauce itself is a harmonious blend of flavors, with hints of sautéed ginger and garlic that add depth and complexity to the dish. Whether enjoyed with steamed rice, roti, or naan bread, curry chicken is a culinary delight that brings together the vibrant flavors of the Caribbean and the exotic spices of India.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • ¾ cup coconut milk
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
    •

Intructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion until lightly browned.
  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt. Continue stirring for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
